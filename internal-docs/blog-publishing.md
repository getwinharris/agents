# Blog publishing contract

`blogs.bapx.in` is the public release, research, tutorial, announcement, and SEO publishing surface. It is not internal technical documentation and must not duplicate `internal-docs/`.

Every post is Markdown with YAML frontmatter under `apps/www/src/content/blogs/` and belongs to exactly one category:

- `announcement` — company, product, partnership, availability, and business announcements;
- `release` — shipped product changes, release notes, migration summaries, and launch posts;
- `research` — bapX findings, experiments, comparisons, and evidence-backed technical or business research;
- `tutorials` — user-facing hosted-product instructions and practical workflows.

Posts may be used as SEO landing content and social/poster campaign sources. SEO content must remain accurate to implemented product behavior, use the approved bapX brand system, and link to the relevant public Platform, MCP, Ecosystem, signup, or product documentation. Do not publish internal CLI/package procedures or describe unfinished controls as available.

Release-facing product changes update `CHANGELOG.md` and the applicable release or announcement post in the same shipping change when public communication is required.
